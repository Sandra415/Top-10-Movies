Cities to Visit while in Poland

City Name Population Popular Attractions
Warszawa 1,758,143 (2017) Old Town (Historic Walking Areas), The 17th-century Ostrogski Castle houses the Chopin Museum and The National Stadium
Kraków 766,739 (2017) Wawel Castle, Wieliczka Salt Mine and Market Square
Gdańsk 458,717 (2016) Ulica Długa (Long Street) and Długi Targ (Long Market), a pedestrian thoroughfare surrounded by buildings reconstructed in historical (primarily during the 17th century) style
Ełk 59,790 (2013) Promenada (Jogging Paths and Tracks), John Paul II Square and The water tower, built in 1895
Zakopane 27,424 (2006) Tatra Mountains (popular with skiers in winter and with hikers and climbers year-round)
